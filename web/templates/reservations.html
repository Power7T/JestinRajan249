▶ What HostAI does with your reservations
Guest context enrichment — When a guest emails you, HostAI matches them to their reservation and includes check-in date, checkout date, and nights stayed in the AI prompt. Replies become far more personal.
Pre-arrival message — 7 days before check-in, a warm welcome draft is auto-generated for your approval.
Check-out thank-you — On checkout day, a thank-you message with a review request is drafted.
Cleaner brief — On checkout day, a full cleaning checklist is drafted for your cleaner.
Review reminder — 14 days after checkout, a nudge to leave the guest a review before the Airbnb window closes.
