Questions? We Have Answers

HostAI works with Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com, direct reservations, and iCal imports. We also support WhatsApp, SMS (via Twilio), and email. Any platform you use, we integrate with.

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're GDPR compliant, SOC2 audited, and we never sell guest data to third parties. Your data is yours only.
