Calendar (Airbnb iCal)

How to find your iCal URL
  1. Go to airbnb.com and open your listing
  2. Click Calendar in the top menu
  3. Click Availability settings
  4. Scroll down and click Export Calendar
  5. Copy the .ics link and paste it above

Email intake

Create an auto-forward rule in Gmail or Outlook for Airbnb guest emails to this address. HostAI will ingest them through a webhook and create drafts without needing your inbox password.

Advanced fallback: connect mailbox with IMAP / SMTP
How to create a Gmail App Password
  1. Go to myaccount.google.com
  2. Click Security in the left menu
  3. Scroll to How you sign in to Google and click 2-Step Verification (must be ON)
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click App passwords
  5. Choose app: Mail, device: Other → type "HostAI" → click Generate
  6. Copy the 16-character password and paste it above

Not using Gmail? Use your provider's equivalent app password or IMAP credentials.

Having trouble? Connection troubleshooting tips →
  • Gmail: Enable IMAP in Gmail Settings → See All Settings → Forwarding and POP/IMAP → IMAP Access → Enable IMAP
  • 2FA enabled: Use an App Password instead of your regular password (see instructions above)
  • Outlook/Office 365: May require enabling IMAP in Outlook settings or using an App Password if legacy auth is disabled
  • "Less secure app" error: Use an App Password — regular password login via IMAP may be blocked
  • Connection timeout: Check that the IMAP host is correct (e.g., imap.gmail.com, outlook.office365.com)
