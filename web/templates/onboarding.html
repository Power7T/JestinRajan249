Step 5 — Connect your channels

HostAI reads your Airbnb emails and replies via SMTP. It also watches your calendar for proactive messages.

Calendar (Airbnb iCal)

How to find your iCal URL
  1. Go to airbnb.com and open your listing
  2. Click Calendar in the top menu
  3. Click Availability settings
  4. Scroll down and click Export Calendar
  5. Copy the .ics link and paste it above

Email (IMAP / SMTP)

How to create a Gmail App Password
  1. Go to myaccount.google.com
  2. Click Security in the left menu
  3. Scroll to How you sign in to Google and click 2-Step Verification (must be ON)
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click App passwords
  5. Choose app: Mail, device: Other → type "HostAI" → click Generate
  6. Copy the 16-character password and paste it above

Not using Gmail? Use your provider's equivalent app password or IMAP credentials.

Claude API key

How to get a free Anthropic API key
  1. Go to console.anthropic.com
  2. Sign up for a free account
  3. Click API Keys in the left sidebar
  4. Click Create Key, give it a name, copy the key

New accounts get $5 free credits. Each message costs about $0.01.
