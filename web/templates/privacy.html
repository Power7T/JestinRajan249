1. Introduction

HostAI ("we", "us", "our" or "Company") operates the HostAI.app website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes:

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, password (hashed), and optional profile information such as property name, location, and contact details. This information is used to provide you with access to the platform and deliver our services.

2.2 Reservation and Guest Data

You may upload reservation data including guest names, phone numbers, email addresses, and check-in/check-out dates. This data is processed to enable draft generation and communication management. Guest information is processed according to your property's data retention settings.

2.3 Communication Data

Messages received from guests through email, WhatsApp, SMS, and other channels are collected, processed, and stored to generate AI drafts and maintain conversation history. You can control data retention through your settings (default: 30 days).

2.4 Usage Data

We collect information on how you interact with the platform (e.g., drafts approved/rejected, features used) for analytics, service improvement, and billing purposes. This includes IP addresses, browser type, and access times.

3. Data Processing and AI

HostAI processes your messages through a third-party AI service (OpenRouter) to generate draft responses. Personal information such as phone numbers and email addresses are redacted before transmission. Property context, reservation details, and guest conversation history are sent to generate contextual drafts. You can review all AI-generated content before sending.

4. Data Retention

By default, data (activity logs, drafts, timeline events) is retained for 30 days. You can adjust this setting in your account. Subscription data and billing records are retained as required by law. Data is automatically purged based on your retention policy.

5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data. When you delete your account, we delete personal information within 30 days, except where required by law. Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days.

6. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide functionality:

  • OpenRouter — AI model provider for draft generation
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Twilio — SMS delivery (if enabled)
  • Meta Cloud API — WhatsApp integration (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We do not share your data with these services beyond what is necessary for platform functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information, including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained in — computers located outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. If you are located outside the US and choose to provide information to us, please note that we may transfer the information to the US and process it there.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@hostai.app.

10. Children's Privacy

HostAI is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@hostai.app
  • By visiting this page on our website: hostai.app/support
