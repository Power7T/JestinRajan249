Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between Baileys and Meta Cloud?

Baileys runs a small bot on your own computer using your personal WhatsApp account. Meta Cloud API uses WhatsApp Business (official Meta product) running entirely on our servers — no software to install, supports high volume.

Do I need to install anything for Baileys?

Just Node.js. After subscribing, you download a pre-configured zip from your Settings page, run one script, and scan a QR code once. That's it.

What happens when my subscription expires?

The messaging channels stop working immediately. Your dashboard, drafts, and data are preserved. Renew anytime to resume.

Can I switch plans?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your billing page. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is free forever. Paid plans offer a 7-day trial — cancel before then and you won't be charged.
