Frequently asked questions

What counts as a "unit"?

One unit = one property listing. If you manage 3 Airbnb properties, you need 3 units.

What's included in all plans?

All plans include full access to every messaging channel: WhatsApp (Baileys and Meta Cloud), SMS via Twilio, and email. There are no channel-based restrictions.

What happens when my subscription expires?

The messaging channels stop working immediately. Your dashboard, drafts, and data are preserved. Renew anytime to resume.

Can I change my unit count?

Yes — update your subscription at any time from the billing page. Price changes take effect immediately and are prorated.
